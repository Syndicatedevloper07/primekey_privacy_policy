Privacy Policy for PrimeKey

Last updated: June 11, 2025

1. Introduction

PrimeKey is a simple, offline password manager that does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. Your passwords are stored securely on your device only.

2. Internet Access for Ads

PrimeKey includes ads for monetization. To serve these advertisements, the app requires internet access.

  • No personal or behavioral data is collected for ad targeting.
  • No user data is transmitted to our servers.

3. Data Collection

PrimeKey does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone)
  • User content (passwords, notes) — all stored locally
  • Analytics, crash reporting, or logging

However, ad networks may collect standard ad-related data (e.g., device IP) solely for delivering ads.

4. Third-Party Ad Providers

Ads are delivered by third-party networks (e.g. AdMob). Their own privacy policies apply. We are not responsible for their data collection practices. You can review them here:

  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Safety & Security

No sensitive data is transmitted off-device by PrimeKey. Internet access is used only to fetch ads.

The app operates entirely on your device. No user data is uploaded, shared, or stored remotely.

6. Your Rights

You can uninstall PrimeKey anytime to remove any app data. No user data is retained by us.
