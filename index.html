Privacy Policy for PrimeKey

Last updated: June 11, 2025

1. Introduction

PrimeKey is a simple, offline password manager that does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. Your passwords are stored securely on your device only.

2. Internet Access for Ads

PrimeKey includes ads for monetization. To serve these advertisements, the app requires internet access.

  • No personal or behavioral data is collected for ad targeting.
  • No user data is transmitted to our servers.

3. Data Collection

PrimeKey does not collect any personal or user-generated data. Your passwords and sensitive content remain stored only on your device.

However, we use Google Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash data to improve app stability and performance. This may include:

  • Device type and OS version
  • Crash stack traces
  • Basic usage info at the time of crash

No personal identifiers or user content are included in these reports.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for both ads and crash analytics:

  • AdMob: To display ads
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To collect crash and diagnostic data

These services may collect device-related data (e.g., IP address, OS version) according to their privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Safety & Security

No sensitive data is transmitted off-device by PrimeKey. Internet access is used only to fetch ads.

The app operates entirely on your device. No user data is uploaded, shared, or stored remotely.

6. Your Rights

You can uninstall PrimeKey anytime to remove any app data. No user data is retained by us.
